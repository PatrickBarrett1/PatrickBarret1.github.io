Cloudiness

Cloudiness does not have a strong relation to latitude. However, cities located between 45 and 60 degrees north or south of the equator tend to experience greater cloudiness. Cloud coverage is much less near the equator since air descends between about 15 and 30 degrees north and south of the equator.
